About Us Page

Welcome! We are the Norwich Testing Initiative (NTI)

Millions have lost their jobs and thousands have lost their lives. We want to put an end to this and prevent Covid-19 from spreading even further. This is why we're here. To provide the ability for everyone to get tested safely, and to help track and trace those who tested positive so others don't get affected.

Work with us to fight this disease together!
